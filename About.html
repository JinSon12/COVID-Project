COVID-19 Tracker

The most up-to-date information on the Corona Virus in the United States.

_______

This project was created for Informatics 340 class project for Spring 2020 by Jin Son and Kyle Kusche at the University of Washington.
This web app will convey the current information of COVID-19 cases in the United States. The users would be able to view both the nation-wide and state-wide statistics of the state of their choice.
The users can click on the row of the table to see a more detailed statistics for their state of choice.
