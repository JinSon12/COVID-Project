What is COVID-19?

COVID-19 in a highly infectious disease which was first found in December 2019 in Wuhan, China. Ever since its discovery, it has been spreading across the globe, causing a pandemic. The incubation period of this virus is estimated to be somewhere around from 2 to 10 days. (WHO) CDC, however, estimated that it would be around 2~14 days. It has recently been discovered that it is possible that the symptoms to not be apparent for 45 days.

Transmission and minimizing the spread

While there has not been many cases reporting the species to species transmission, it is speculated that humans can spread the virus to other species, with feline species being slightly more suceptible to virus than other species.
